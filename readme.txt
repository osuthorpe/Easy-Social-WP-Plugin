A simple to use social media plugin with Facebook, Google+, Twitter, Linkedin & Stumbleupon post buttons and a widget. 

== Description ==

Ever get tired of having a different plugin for each social media site or having one with so many options you get configured? The Easy Social Plugin was designed with you in mind. After you activate the plugin just enter your information into a few fields, place the easy social widget where you want it to appear and your done.

After each post you will have buttons so your visitors can share your content on Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, Google+ & Stumbleupon while the widget gives you links back to all your social media sites.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `easy-social` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings => Easy Social Settings and input your social media information
4. Place the Easy Social widget in the desired widget area
5. Enjoy!
